Kitchen
Oven
An oven is a thermally insulated chamber used for the heating, baking, or drying of a substance, and most commonly used for cooking.
Refrigerator
A refrigerator is a machine for keeping things cold. It is sometimes called a fridge.
Island
An island is a freestanding piece of cabinetry that is placed in a kitchen to supplement the countertop space.

Living Room
Sofa
A long, upholstered couch with a back and two arms or raised ends.
Television
Television is a telecommunication medium used for transmitting moving images in monochrome (black and white), or in colour, and in two or three dimensions and sound.
